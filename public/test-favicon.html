Manual Cache Clearing Steps:

  1. Chrome/Edge: Press Ctrl+Shift+Delete, select "Cached images and files", click "Clear data"
  2. Firefox: Press Ctrl+Shift+Delete, select "Cache", click "Clear Now"
  3. Safari: Go to Safari > Preferences > Advanced, check "Show Develop menu", then Develop > Empty Caches
  4. Alternative: Try opening this page in an incognito/private window

Note: Sometimes you need to close ALL tabs of the site and reopen them for the favicon to update.
